Together referred to as 'the Parties,' both agree to the following terms and conditions:
1. Purpose of Agreement
The Consignor wishes to place certain items ('Consigned Goods') with Bunyards Coin and Stamps for sale on consignment. The store will display and offer the items for sale to customers on behalf of the Consignor under the terms described herein.
2. Consignment Fee & Pricing

The Consignor shall set the desired net price for each consigned item. Bunyards Coin and Stamps will add a 10% commission to that price to cover the cost of selling, display, and in-store holding of the item.

Consignor will agree to their product having a 10% markup to cover the consignment agreement, OR 10% subtracted from their desired price.
EXAMPLE: If Consignor wants a firm $90 for their item, 10% is added as retail. Total = $100 — $10 (10%) to the store, $90 to the Consignor.

The Consignor acknowledges that this 10% fee is non-negotiable and covers all costs associated with displaying, securing, and selling the item within the store.

3. Precious Metals & Price Updates

The Consignor understands that the market value of gold, silver, and other precious metals fluctuates daily. As a result:

  • The Consignor is responsible for monitoring current market prices for their consigned items.
  • The Consignor MUST provide updated pricing at least once every fourteen (14) days for items containing gold, silver, or other commodity metals.
  • Price updates must be submitted in writing on the Consignment Price Update Sheet.
  • Failure to update within the 14-day window may result in temporary removal from display.
  • Bunyards Coin and Stamps is not responsible for loss in value due to failure to update pricing timely.
4. Loss, Theft & Liability

The store maintains secured display cases, security cameras, and staff supervision. However:

  • Bunyards Coin and Stamps CANNOT guarantee absolute security against theft, fire, flood, or other unforeseen events.
  • In the event of theft or loss despite reasonable precautions, the store shall not be held financially liable.
  • Consignors are strongly encouraged to maintain their own insurance coverage for high-value items.
  • The store will cooperate fully with law enforcement and provide all available footage and records in the event of a theft.
5. Term & Removal of Items
Items remain in the store for a minimum of thirty (30) days. After 30 days the Consignor may request return of unsold items with reasonable notice. The store reserves the right to return items with 7 days' written notice.
6. Payment to Consignor
Upon sale, payment of the Consignor's agreed net amount will be issued within seven (7) business days by cash or check as preferred, unless other arrangements are made.
7. Condition of Items
The Consignor warrants all items are their lawful property, free of liens or encumbrances, and that they have full authority to consign the items. Items are accepted in the condition described on the Consignment Item Sheet.
8. Agreement to Terms
By signing below, both parties acknowledge they have read, understood, and agree to all terms of this Consignment Agreement.
